## STEP 1: Upload Your Files via GitHub Web UI

### Upload Thumbnails (5 Images)

1. **Go to your repository:**
   - Visit: https://github.com/pokemonjay364-droid/PrevailUnbroken
   - Make sure you're on the `rise-above-shorts` branch

2. **Navigate to the thumbnails folder:**
   - Click on `rise-above-shorts` folder
   - Click on `assets` folder
   - Click on `thumbnails` folder

3. **Upload your images:**
   - Click "Add file" → "Upload files"
   - Drag and drop your 5 thumbnail images, OR click "choose your files"
   - **IMPORTANT: Use exact filenames:**
     - `01-3-second-rule.jpg`
     - `02-worst-day-teacher.jpg`
     - `03-fall-7-rise-8.jpg`
     - `04-bamboo-tree-secret.jpg`
     - `05-you-are-stronger.jpg`

4. **Commit your changes:**
   - Add commit message: "Add thumbnail images for video series"
   - Click "Commit changes"

### Upload PDF Guide

2. **Upload your PDF:**
   - Click "Add file" → "Upload files"

### Upload Video Files (5 MP4s)

1. **Navigate to the exports folder:**
   - Go back to: https://github.com/pokemonjay364-droid/PrevailUnbroken
   - Click `rise-above-shorts` → `exports`

2. **Upload your videos:**
   - Click "Add file" → "Upload files"
   - Upload your 5 MP4 video files
   - **IMPORTANT: Use exact filenames:**
     - `01-the-3-second-rule.mp4`
     - `02-your-worst-day-is-your-best-teacher.mp4`
     - `03-fall-7-times-rise-8.mp4`
     - `04-the-bamboo-tree-secret.mp4`
     - `05-you-are-stronger-than-you-know.mp4`

3. **Commit your changes:**
   - Add commit message: "Add video files for the series"
   - Click "Commit changes"

**Note:** If your video files are very large (>100MB), GitHub may have size limits. Consider:
- Compressing videos first
- Using GitHub's Large File Storage (LFS)
- Hosting videos on Vimeo/YouTube and linking instead

## STEP 2: Enable GitHub Pages

1. **Go to Repository Settings:**
   - Navigate to: https://github.com/pokemonjay364-droid/PrevailUnbroken
   - Click the **Settings** tab (top right)

2. **Click Pages (left sidebar):**
   - Look for "Pages" in the left navigation menu
   - Click it

3. **Configure Source:**
   - Under "Build and deployment" section:
   - Select: **Deploy from a branch**
   - Branch dropdown: Select **main** (or your default branch)
   - Folder dropdown: Select **/ (root)**
   - Click **Save**

4. **Wait for Deployment:**
   - GitHub will show a message: "Your site is live at..."
   - This typically takes 1-2 minutes
   - You can check the progress in the "Deployments" section

### Testing Checklist:

- [ ] **Landing page loads** - All text and styling display correctly
- [ ] **Header gradient** appears (blue to orange)
- [ ] **Thumbnails load** - All 5 video cards show images
- [ ] **Play buttons work** - Hover over video cards, see "Watch" button
- [ ] **Video player opens** - Click a play button, modal appears
- [ ] **Video plays** - Video loads and plays correctly (if you uploaded MP4s)
- [ ] **PDF downloads** - "Download Guide" button works
- [ ] **Responsive design** - Test on mobile (landscape & portrait)
- [ ] **Links work** - All buttons and links function correctly

### Common Issues & Fixes:

| Issue | Solution |
|-------|----------|
| Site shows 404 | Wait 2-3 minutes for GitHub to build. Refresh page. |
| Thumbnails don't load | Check filenames match exactly (case-sensitive on GitHub) |
| Videos don't play | Verify MP4 files are in `exports/` folder with correct names |
| PDF won't download | Check filename: `Overcoming-Shorts-Series-Companion-Guide.pdf` |
| Styling looks broken | Clear browser cache (Ctrl+Shift+Delete) and refresh |
| Mobile looks squished | Zoom to 100%. Site is fully responsive. |

4. **Cross-promote:** Mention other platforms in each post
5. **Ask questions:** Every caption should end with an engagement prompt
6. **Use stories/reels:** Short-form content gets more reach
7. **Go live:** Host live Q&As on Instagram/TikTok/Facebook
8. **Collaborate:** Tag complementary creators in your posts
9. **Update bio:** Make sure your bio links to your series
10. **Be consistent:** Post at least 3x per week to build momentum
